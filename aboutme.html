About

Yella, age 20, is a multifaceted individual with Nigerian heritage who grew up in Essex and East London, UK.

Over the years, she has cultivated a range of diverse interests and talents, including skateboarding for 3 years, martial arts in Kickboxing and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu for a total of 6 years, modeling, pole dancing, and becoming a software engineer by 20.

In 2021 she was diagnosed with ADHD. Despite being neurodiverse, Yella harnesses her unique strengths to fuel her passion for learning, creativity, and exploring new avenues of interest.
